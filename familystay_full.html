Our story

We built the site
we desperately needed.

We are a family of four. Two parents, two children, and an embarrassingly long list of hotel stays that did not go as planned.

We have shown up to a resort where the kids club was closed for the season — information that was nowhere on the booking page. We have spent entire evenings cross-referencing TripAdvisor, hotel websites, and parent forums trying to answer one simple question: does this hotel actually work for a family? We have paid a fortune for places marketed as family resorts that turned out to be adult pools with a single waterslide tucked behind the car park.

Every single time, we thought the same thing: there has to be a better way.

Our promise

We built this because we were the ones with 14 tabs open at midnight.

Every hotel here earned its place by actually being good for families — real kids clubs, real babysitting, real waterparks, not a checkbox on a filter. If it wouldn't work for our own kids, it's not on the list.
